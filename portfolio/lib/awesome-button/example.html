Web components are awesome

The awesome-button is a cool option to quickly add share buttons (or any kind of buttons) to your projects.

<awesome-button/>

  • <awesome-button type="facebook" sharer>Share it!</awesome-button>
    Share it!
  • <awesome-button type="gplus" sharer>Share it!</awesome-button>
    Share it!
  • <awesome-button type="twitter" sharer></awesome-button>
  • <awesome-button type="github" href="http://github.com/">Fork it!</awesome-button>
    Fork it!
  • <awesome-button type="external" target="_blank" href="http://caferati.me/labs/awesome-button">Try these buttons</awesome-button>
    Try these buttons
